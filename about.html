ABOUT

We are building a decentralized intelligence network for real-world environmental monitoring powered by AI, satellite data, and blockchain infrastructure. Our focus is creating a scalable system that can analyze satellite imagery and geospatial data in real time to detect environmental risks, verify events, and generate reliable insights for industries such as insurance, climate, agriculture, and carbon markets.

Built on top of the Bittensor ecosystem, our subnet incentivizes miners to produce high-quality environmental intelligence while validators continuously verify accuracy, reliability, and resistance to manipulation. Our mission is to transform massive amounts of raw Earth observation data into actionable, trustless, and economically aligned intelligence.

We believe the future of environmental intelligence should be open, decentralized, and autonomous. Today, critical climate and infrastructure decisions depend on fragmented data providers, opaque models, and slow verification systems.

Our vision is to build a decentralized layer for real-time planetary monitoring — a network where AI models compete to generate the most accurate understanding of what is happening on Earth.

From wildfire detection and climate risk analysis to agricultural monitoring and carbon verification, we aim to create infrastructure that enables faster decisions, transparent validation, and globally accessible environmental intelligence.

We are a team of builders, researchers, and engineers working at the intersection of AI, crypto, geospatial systems, and decentralized infrastructure. Our background combines:

* Blockchain and decentralized systems development
* AI and machine learning engineering
* Satellite and geospatial data analysis
* Product design and infrastructure architecture
* Crypto-native network and incentive design

We are deeply focused on building production-grade decentralized systems that solve real-world problems with measurable impact.
